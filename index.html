Badgers & Gnomes

There are 3 entity types:

Cabbage Gnomes Badger

Badgers eat gnomes. Gnomes will eat cabbages while evading badgers.

All entities will lose health over time. When their health reaches 0, they will die.

Controls:

Spacebar: Pause/Resume

Mouse click: Create entity

1-3: Select entity to create

What is this?

A playground of independent agents controlled by weighted vectors.
